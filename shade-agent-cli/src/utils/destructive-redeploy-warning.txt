    balances, registrations, configs, or other persisted data).

    recovered after deletion.

You may also not be able to redeploy to the same account name afterwards.

Before continuing, confirm that:
  - The contract holds no valuable state you need to preserve.
  - All fungible tokens, NFTs, and other assets associated with this
    account have already been migrated to a different account.

By typing "yes" you acknowledge the loss of all account state and assets
and choose to proceed with the redeploy.
